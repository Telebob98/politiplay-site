// The game

How it works

1
Take your seat
You're assigned to a table of ten. These are your opponents — nine people who think they read the political room just as well as you do. Your seat is yours for the season.
2
Draft your squad
Pick ten real public figures — two per position across HoS, LEG, OPP, WBR, and WLD. From a catalog of 752 world leaders, power brokers, Kibitzers, and Fourth Estate figures.
3
Score the cycle
When your figures drive headlines, you score. Every point is editorially reviewed, sourced, and linked. One swap before Friday midnight. Sweep closes Sunday. Best read wins.
// Why it matters

A judgment arena.
Not just a game.

Everyone has opinions about politics. Most people never have to back them up. Politiplay changes that. Every draft pick is a statement of belief — who's going to move, who's going to matter, who's about to blow up the cycle.

"Your political instincts, tested weekly against the room and against the news."

The score reflects the news cycle, adjudicated against Politiplay's published rules. Every point sourced and linked. When a Kibitzer surfaces in a document leak and scores 20 points nobody saw coming — that's the player who was paying attention.
